Know more...


Campsite Occupancy Survey is a monthly situation report that collects information on the supply and demand for tourist campsites in Andalusia. The main variables studied are: the category of the campsite, travellers lodged, sites occupied (or overnight stays), campsite capacity, staff employed, occupancy level, average stay, country and region of residence.
